    Sausage English Breakfast

    1. Prepare Sausages: Start by frying high-quality pork or beef sausages in a pan until they're golden brown and cooked through.

    2. Cook Eggs: Fry, scramble, or poach eggs according to your preference.

    3. Prepare Bacon: Cook strips of back or streaky bacon in a pan until crispy.

    4. Toast Bread: Toast slices of white or wholegrain bread and spread with butter.

    5. Grill Tomatoes: Cut tomatoes in half, season with herbs, and grill until tender.

    6. Sauté Mushrooms: Sauté mushrooms in butter with a touch of garlic until they're soft and flavorful.

    7. Cook Black Pudding: Slice black pudding (blood sausage) and fry it until it's heated through.

    8. Prepare Baked Beans: Heat a small serving of baked beans to serve on the side.

    9. Serve with Hash Browns: Accompany the breakfast with crispy fried hash browns.

    Beans English Breakfast

    1. Prepare Baked Beans: Begin by heating a can of quality baked beans in a saucepan over medium heat.

    2. Cook Eggs: Fry, scramble, or poach eggs to your liking.

    3. Grill Tomatoes: Halve tomatoes, season with salt, pepper, and a little oil, and grill until soft.

    4. Sauté Mushrooms: Cook mushrooms in a pan with butter and a hint of garlic until golden.

    5. Toast Bread: Toast slices of your preferred bread and butter them while warm.

    6. Prepare Hash Browns: Fry hash browns in oil until they are crispy and golden brown.

    7. Cook Vegetarian Sausages: If including, fry vegetarian sausages according to package instructions.

    8. Serve Breakfast: Arrange all components on a plate, ensuring a generous serving of baked beans.

    9. Beverage: Accompany the breakfast with a cup of tea or coffee.
