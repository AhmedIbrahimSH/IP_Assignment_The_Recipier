    Pasta

    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.

    2. Add your choice of pasta to the boiling water.

    3. Cook the pasta for 8-10 minutes or until al dente.

    4. In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.

    5. Sauté minced garlic and chopped onions in the pan until translucent.

    6. Add a can of crushed tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper to the pan.

    7. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

    8. Drain the pasta and combine it with the sauce in the pan.

    9. Toss the pasta well to ensure it’s fully coated with the sauce.

    10. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
