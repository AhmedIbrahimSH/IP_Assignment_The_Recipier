    Burger

    1. Start by preheating your grill or pan to medium-high heat.

    2. Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and your choice of seasonings.

    3. Form the mixture into patties, about ½ inch thick.

    4. Place the patties on the grill or pan, cooking for about 4 minutes on each side for medium-rare.

    5. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute until lightly crispy.

    6. Prepare your toppings: slice tomatoes, lettuce, onions, and pickles.

    7. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.

    8. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the buns and adding your toppings and condiments.

    9. Optionally, add ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise to enhance the flavor.

    10. Serve the burger hot, accompanied by fries or a salad.
