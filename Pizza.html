    Pizza Recipe

    1. Start by preparing the pizza dough. Combine yeast, water, and sugar in a bowl and let it sit for 5 minutes.

    2. Add flour and salt to the yeast mixture. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth.

    3. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

    4. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

    5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

    6. Spread a layer of pizza sauce over the dough. Add a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.

    7. Add your favorite toppings, keeping it simple for a classic cheese pizza.

    8. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

    9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and serve.
